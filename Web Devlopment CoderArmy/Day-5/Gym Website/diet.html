Sample 7-Day Meal Plan (Overview)

Below are sample days for three primary goals — you can repeat these for 7 days or add variety.

Weight Loss — Sample Day (Approx 1500 kcal)

Meal Food Items Approx Calories Notes
Breakfast Oats (1 cup) with skim milk + 1 banana + 1 tsp honey 350 kcal High-fiber, slow carbs
Mid-Morning Snack Greek yogurt (100 g) + handful almonds (10) 180 kcal Protein + healthy fats
Lunch Grilled chicken (150 g) + mixed salad + 1 chapati or 1/2 cup brown rice 400 kcal Lean protein + veggies
Evening Snack Apple + peanut butter (1 tbsp) 150 kcal Satiety before workout
Dinner Steamed fish or tofu + steamed veggies + small sweet potato 350 kcal Light and protein-rich
Before Sleep (optional) Warm milk (200 ml) or cottage cheese (50 g) 70 kcal Slow-release protein

Muscle Gain — Sample Day (Approx 3000 kcal)

Meal Food Items Approx Calories Notes
Breakfast 3 eggs omelette + 2 slices wholegrain bread + avocado 650 kcal Protein + healthy fats
Mid-Morning Smoothie: banana + milk + whey (1 scoop) + oats 400 kcal Quick calories & protein
Lunch Brown rice (1.5 cups) + chicken breast (200 g) + veggies 800 kcal Carb + protein heavy
Pre-Workout Snack Peanut butter sandwich or dates + nuts 300 kcal Energy for training
Post-Workout Whey shake + banana 250 kcal Fast protein & carbs
Dinner Salmon (200 g) + quinoa + salad 600 kcal Protein & healthy fats

Diet Tips & Guidelines

  • Drink 2–3 liters of water daily.
  • Prioritize protein in each meal for muscle repair and satiety.
  • Include vegetables and fiber for digestion and fullness.
  • Control portion sizes and avoid late-night heavy meals.
  • Use whole foods over processed foods as much as possible.
  • Adjust calories gradually — don't make sudden large cuts.
